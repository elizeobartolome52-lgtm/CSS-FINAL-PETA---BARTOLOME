Marikina launches daily operations for cleaner city

The Marikina City Government, under the direction of Mayor Maan Teodoro, continues its daily Inter-Agency Task Force operations to maintain cleanliness, safety, and order throughout the city.

Teams are dispatched to various areas to address infrastructure and public space concerns, including tree cutting, wire bundling, streetlight maintenance, sidewalk and road repairs, and repainting of vandalized walls. These efforts aim to create more orderly and well-maintained communities for residents.

The operations cover all barangays and involve coordinated action among multiple city departments, ensuring that maintenance and beautification work are carried out efficiently and sustainably.

City officials also use the opportunity to educate residents on proper environmental stewardship, encouraging responsible behavior and active participation in keeping the city clean and organized.

Through this sustained initiative, Marikina demonstrates its commitment to providing safe, accessible, and visually appealing public spaces for all citizens.
