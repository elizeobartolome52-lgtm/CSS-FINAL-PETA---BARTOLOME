Marikina recognizes outstanding employees

The City Government of Marikina recognized exceptional public servants during the MARIKINA PRAISE Awards, the city’s Program on Awards and Incentives for Service Excellence.

Among those honored were recipients of the Gantimpala Agad Award, which is given to employees praised by clients for their prompt, efficient, and excellent service. Awardees included Dr. Garnel Javier, Wilhelmina Salas, and Rebecca Espiritu from Barangay Health Center – Concepcion Dos, and Lolita Mariano from the City Health Office, all commended for their dedication and professionalism.

The program also recognized exemplary employees through the Model Employee Award. Mr. Rolando Salazar received the award under the Level 1 Category, while Mr. Christopher S. Hermogenes was honored under the Level 2 Category for their outstanding and model service to the city.

City officials highlighted that these awards reflect the city government’s commitment to encouraging excellence, accountability, and a culture of public service among employees.

By acknowledging and celebrating their employees’ achievements, Marikina continues to inspire its workforce to maintain high standards in serving the community.
