FUN FACTS ABOUT MARIKINA


  1. Marikina was originally called "Mariquina," named after a local river. In 1571, Spanish explorer Martin de Goiti founded it. The city is famous for producing quality shoes since the 1800s. In 1996, Marikina was declared as a "Highly Urbanized City" because of its rapid growth and development. Today, the city still honors its rich history through events and celebrations.
  2. Marikina, known as the "Shoe Capital of the Philippines," produces nearly 70% of the country's shoes and houses the world's largest pair of shoes.
  3. Marikina is frequently recognized as one of the cleanest and most organized cities in Metro Manila, often winning local and international awards for its urban planning and disaster resilience.
