Marikina holds free medical, dental mission

The Marikina City government will conduct a free medical and dental mission as part of its continuing effort to expand healthcare services for residents.

The activity will be held on March 14, 2026, from 7:00 a.m. to 12:00 noon at Malanday Elementary School, while another session is scheduled on March 15, 2026, at Fortune High School.

The second day of the mission will be exclusively for women in line with the celebration of National Women’s Month.

The initiative forms part of the health programs promoted by Marcy Teodoro, aimed at providing accessible healthcare services to families in the city.

Residents who attend the mission may avail of free medical consultations, dental services, and medicines as the city government continues to promote a healthier and more vibrant community.
